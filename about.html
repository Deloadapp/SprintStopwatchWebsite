About

Hey, I'm Mustafa and I'm studying human technology in Cologne 👋🏼.

For the past years, I’ve been using my iPhone’s stopwatch to time athletes’ runs—it’s quick and convenient, but not ideal for tracking multiple sprints at once.
It’s easy to miss the stop button, and I got tired of constantly doing the math on lap times. Writing everything down takes extra time, and in the hustle of training, I often start a new note each time—losing track of past results just when I need them🫠

I always thought there had to be a better way, so while procrastinating on my bachelor’s thesis, I started piecing together some code. It quickly became a passion project, and now I’m thrilled to share this app with you!✨

Since this is a hobby project, I’m offering the app for free to make it accessible to as many people as possible. I hope it becomes a valuable tool in your coaching!⏱️
